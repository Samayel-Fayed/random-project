Sanko Optical Co. (BD) Ltd.

Sanko Optical Co., (BD) Ltd. Are planning to set up in 1992 in the Chittagong export processing zone. Sanko Optical Co. (BD) Ltd. was established on 1992, as an associated company in the Chittagong export processing zone of Plot No: 20, 21 and 22 at Sector No: 6 in Chittagong, Bangladesh. The company presently occupied total 6000 sq. meter. In which 2476 sq. meter for production area. At the beginning, the amount of this registered capital was of us $ 600,000 which now has risen up to us$ 32,00,000. The company started its first production on May 5th, 1992, and made its first export to JAPAN, on June 5th, 1992. Camera, Video Camera, Fax, Photo copy and Security Camera (cctv) lenses etc. are made in this company too.
